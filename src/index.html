What is the EPP?

During the first 2 weeks of class, students are able to take an in-lab programming exam. This is the Exam in Programming Proficiency (EPP). During the exam students will be required to write programs and documentation on the computer. For more details on the EPP, please visit the EPP Exam Information & Resource Files webpage.

If you're going to be taking the EPP, you should consider attending the ACM EPP Review. ACM CSUF hosts review sessions for the EPP the weekend before the EPP takes place. Keep an eye out for an email from the Campus Emailer for more details!
